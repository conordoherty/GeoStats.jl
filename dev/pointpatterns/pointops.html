Operations

Below is the list of currently implemented operations for point processes and patterns.

Superposition

The union or (superposition) of two point processes creates a union process:

Base.union — Method
p₁ ∪ p₂

Return the union of point processes p₁ and p₂.

Thinning

The thin function implements the thinning operation for point processes and patterns. Below are the available thinning methods.

PointPatterns.thin — Function
thin(p, t)

Thin spatial point process p with thinning method t.

PointPatterns.RandomThinning — Type
RandomThinning(p)

Random thining with retention probability p.
